Tips

This page lists tips on particular aspects of working with Audacity. Following these will greatly enhance your day-to-day experience of using Audacity.
  • Navigation Tips

How to navigate the waveform or tracks (including moving the playback position) identifying audio for editing, and how to find and mark edit points.

  • Playback Tips

How to view and adjust playback volume, and achieve smooth playback quality.

  • Audio Alignment Tips

How to align multiple tracks so that their audio starts at the same point on the timeline above the waveform

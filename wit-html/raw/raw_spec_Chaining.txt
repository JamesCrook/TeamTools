Interactive diagrams are programs.  This is the program structure for the interactive diagram which shows who is who at the AU19 Audacity conference:

<pre>
VERTICAL_LAYOUT
  RGBA_FOCUS_DETAIL Focus=SmallCircle
</pre>

The specification above builds a simple focus-detail display.  There's a
stand-alone [https://wit.audacityteam.org/demos.htm?page=Maps
interactive demo] but here is the result in wiki:

-----
{{#widget:Dynamic|page=Maps|height=620px}}
-----

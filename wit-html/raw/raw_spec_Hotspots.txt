This is the spec for the interactive diagram which shows who is who at the AU19 Audacity conference - and also explains how the hotspots work:

<pre>
DISPLAY_FOCUS_DETAIL Focus=SmallCircle
  BUTTON_ROW
  IMAGE_WITH_HOTSPOTS
</pre>



The specification above builds a simple focus-detail display.  There's an [https://wit.audacityteam.org/imagemap2_test.htm?page=Hotspots interactive demo].
Based on [[Toolbox/The_AU19_Crowd]]

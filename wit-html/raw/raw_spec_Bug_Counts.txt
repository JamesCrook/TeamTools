Interactive diagrams are programs.  This is the program structure for the interactive diagram which shows a chart of Audacity bug counts:

<pre>
VERTICAL_LAYOUT
  RGBA_FOCUS_DETAIL Focus=SmallCircle
</pre>

The specification above builds a simple focus-detail display.

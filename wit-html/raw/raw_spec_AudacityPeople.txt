This is the spec for the interactive diagram which shows who is who at the AU19 Audacity conference:

<pre>
DISPLAY_FOCUS_DETAIL Focus=SmallCircle
  IMAGE_WITH_HOTSPOTS
</pre>


The specification above builds a simple focus-detail display.  There's a stand-alone [https://wit.audacityteam.org/imagemap2_test.htm?page=The_AU19_Crowd interactive demo] but here is the result in wiki:
